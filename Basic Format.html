How to make a "Power" sandwich

  1. Gather ingredients: Bread, Mayonaise (White), Tomatoes (Red), Mustard (Yellow), Black Olives (Black), Bolony (Pink), Lettuce (Green)
  2. Wash produce
  3. Spread Mayonaise on one Bread slice
  4. Spread Mustard on second slice
  5. Place Bolony on Mayonaise side
  6. Place Lettuce on Bolony
  7. Sprinkle Black Olives on Lettuce
  8. Place 2 Slices of Tomato over Black Olives
  9. Place second slice of Bread over the sandwich
  10. Enjoy with a cold Blue Gatorade!
